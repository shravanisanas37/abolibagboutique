About Aboli Boutique

Our Heritage, Passion, and Commitment

Our Brand Origin Story

Aboli Bag Boutique & Jewellery Shop was born out of a deep passion for elegant, handcrafted fashion accessories that define modern Indian womanhood.

Established in the heart of Satara, we realized that accessories are not merely add-ons—they express personality and style. We began our journey with handcrafted bags, partnering with regional artisans to source traditional weaves, Zardosi work, and rich textures. Over the years, we expanded into designer jewellery to become a one-stop-shop for bridal and everyday fashion statements.

"Aboli is not just about selling products; it is about keeping traditional handicraft alive, making every woman feel regal on her special day."

We pride ourselves on an offline-first policy. We believe true luxury lies in touch, feel, and detailed customization. By hosting physical collections, we provide a warm, tactile environment where you can match accessories to your sarees, dresses, and lehengas in real-time.

Our Mission

To empower women by providing high-quality, handcrafted fashion bags and exquisite jewellery that combine ethnic heritage with contemporary design, all at affordable pricing.

Our Vision

To become Satara's most trusted boutique destination for custom styling and gifting accessories, while supporting local artisans and reviving artisanal techniques.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clarifications about our offline business concept

Can I purchase products directly from this website?
No. Aboli Bag Boutique operates as an offline boutique in Satara. Our website functions as an online display catalog. To buy any of the featured products, please visit our physical shop where you can feel the quality and verify sizes.
Can I check if a product is currently in stock before visiting?
Yes! Simply click the "Enquire on WhatsApp" button on any product card or details modal. It will generate a pre-filled WhatsApp message specifying the item name. We will immediately check our store shelves and reply to you.
Where is your shop located?
Our boutique is located at Shop no 5&6, Laxmi vishnu nivas bldg, Beside Narkar jewellers, opp City centre, Moti chowk, Satara 415002. You can view the interactive map in the contact section below.
What are your boutique hours?
We are open Monday through Sunday (all days) from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Special festival timings or holiday changes are updated on our Google Business Profile page.
Do you offer custom designs for bags and jewelry?
Yes, we do custom bridal potlis and coordinated handbag-jewelry sets for events. Please contact us via WhatsApp or visit our store to discuss your requirements, material selection, and delivery times.
What payment methods do you accept at the store?
We accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards, and cash payments at our Satara billing desk.
What is your return and exchange policy?
Since products are purchased offline after direct inspection, we support standard size/pattern exchanges within 7 days of purchase. The item must be unused, with original tags intact, and accompanied by the store receipt.
Are the materials of your bags durable?
Absolutely. We source high-grade vegan leathers, sturdy brass zippers, cotton canvas, and durable inner linings. Proper care instructions are provided for all handcrafted products inside the details modal.
